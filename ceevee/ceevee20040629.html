Curriculum Vitae

Kris Buytaert

Summary

Kris Buytaert is a long time Linux, Security and Open Source consultant, He has consulting and development experience with multiple enterprise level clients and government agencies. Next to his high level technical experience he is a team leader who likes to deliver his projects on time. He is a contributor to the Linux Documentation Project and author of different technical publications. Kris is a Red Hat Certified Engineer. He is currently the maintainer of the openMosix HOWTO. Kris is also a frequent speaker at Linux and OpenSource conferences. He is currently focusssing Linux Clustering (both HA and HPC), Virtualisation and Large Infrastructure Management.
