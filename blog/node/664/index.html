Why does Gimv invest in OpenBravo

I`m having a bit of a double feeling about the OpenBravo funding from the Gimv. On one hand it's great to see the Gimv invest in Open Source, on the other hand I`m wondering why Spain when there is lots of things going on in Belgium. We have different ERP platforms, a bunch of CMS Systems and much more ... so why must they invest abroad ?

Not all of these companies might be looking for funding, but I`m sure there are some wouldn't mind :)
