#5 Koen Van der Auwera : Awel, let me tell you about

Awel, let me tell you about the Bus by SMS thingie :)

Send a SMS with "DLA" to 4884. Only proximus for the time being. The first time it's free. The second time you have to pay so you have to make sure "M-PAY" is actived on your SIM. The activation is handled automatically after you send the second SMS and this takes about a day.

I suggest you make sure M-PAY is activated before you actually want to use it otherwise you have to wait (and it won't be working).
