Luckily I catched the important parts. The virtualbox talk disappointed me .. this was a marketing talk for endusers, not a talk suitable for Fosdem :( </p> <p><a href="http://pascal.vanhecke.info/" rel="nofollow">Pascal</a> learned that integration Amazon basically is a fine dns problem :) Then after some chatter with the MySQL crowd I headed into the Conary talk.. I was expecting a bit more information around their rbuilder system<br /> I wondered into too much talks on sunday afternoon . the <a href="http://jan.kneschke.de/projects/mysql/mysql-proxy/" rel="nofollow">MySQL Proxy talk</a> , the end of <a href="http://atog.be/2008/2/24/shoes-fosdem" rel="nofollow">atogs</a> talk which he didn't want to repeat :) <a href="http://karan.org/blog/" rel="nofollow">Karan's</a> talk .. etc.</p> <p>I had to miss Simon's talk about Posgtress HA on sunday for the obvious reason , but luckily I could catch him on saturday to get a short sneak preview.. </p> <p>But more about those obvious reasons in a separate post :)</p> <p>My Pics are over <a href="http://flickr.com/photos/77075808@N00/tags/fosdem2008/" rel="nofollow">here</a>.
